Connector - Developing a B2B Proposition

Overview

Connector solves the problem of how to share your contact information and keep it updated.

Connector is very consumer focused, but companies also have problems sharing and managing employees information. In most companies there is a lot of duplication of employee information creating inefficency and innacuracy. Is there an easy way for you to view and correct the address your employer has on your record? Would HR automatically update the accounts department?

For Connector to work for business it would need to facilitate the sharing of contacts. In order to illustrate how this might work I've sketched out how an employee directory might work.

Summary

This information could be used to make important decisions about how the Marley Spoon offering evolves and to identify prospective clients for new products.
